== Description ==
Woo bulk edit price is a plugin that is created to help you edit price of your 100s of products within few minutes. You don't have to open a hundred pages to update prices, you can update cost price and selling price of your products very easily by just typing it, you dons't even have to click on the save button the ajax will take care of it by itself. 




== Installation ==
Upload the plugin to your site and visit page "Bulk edit price" from the WP admin dashboard.
